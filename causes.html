Health Issues Due to Gadget Use

Eye Strain

Staring at screens for long periods leads to eye discomfort, dryness, and headaches.

Neck and Back Pain

Improper posture and prolonged sitting result in musculoskeletal problems.

Sleep Disturbances

Blue light exposure at night affects the natural sleep-wake cycle, causing insomnia.

Sedentary Lifestyle

Extended gadget use encourages inactivity, which can lead to obesity and other health issues.

Repetitive Strain Injuries (RSI)

Frequent typing and touchscreen use can cause strain injuries to the wrists, fingers, and thumbs.

Mental Health Impacts

Excessive social media and news consumption contribute to anxiety, stress, and mental fatigue.

Exposure to Electromagnetic Radiation

Electronic devices emit low levels of radiation, which may have long-term health effects.

Reduced Physical Activity

Prolonged gadget use can replace physical activities, contributing to weight gain and poor muscle tone.

Disrupted Circadian Rhythm

Blue light from screens at night affects melatonin production, disrupting natural sleep cycles.

Digital Eye Strain (Computer Vision Syndrome)

Long screen hours cause eye discomfort, blurred vision, and headaches.

Increased Stress from Information Overload

Constant exposure to information can lead to mental fatigue and difficulty relaxing.
